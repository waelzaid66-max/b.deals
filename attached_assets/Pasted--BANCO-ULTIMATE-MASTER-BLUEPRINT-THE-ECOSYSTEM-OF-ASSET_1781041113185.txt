# 🚀 BANCO ULTIMATE MASTER BLUEPRINT: THE ECOSYSTEM OF ASSETS

## I. SYSTEM PHILOSOPHY: THE ECONOMIC INFRASTRUCTURE
BANCO is not a classifieds app; it is a B2B/B2C infrastructure bridging high-value assets (Cars, Real Estate, Industrial) with structured financing. The system operates on:
1. **Behavioral Velocity:** Decision speed > Decorative UI.
2. **Economic Transparency:** Dual-mode financing logic (Islamic vs. Conventional).
3. **Operational Integrity:** Transaction-led, ledger-based accountability for every interaction.

## II. THE USER PSYCHOLOGY LAYER (The UX Engine)
- **Home Feed (The Hook):** - **Visual Hierarchy:** Image/Video (70%) > Price (20%) > Installment (10%).
    - **Invisible Segmentation:** Feed dynamically switches between 'Hook' (Deals), 'Trust' (Verified), and 'Retarget' (Recent views).
    - **Micro-Triggers:** Data-driven conversion markers ("X people contacted recently").
- **Search Behavior:** - **NLP-First:** Support for "BMW قسط" or "شقة 3 نوم". Auto-suggests and classifies intent instantly.
    - **Filter Priority:** Price, Location, and Installment are 'Top-Level' chips. Everything else is secondary.
- **Listing & Conversion:**
    - **Psychology:** Big price, crossed old price, "Best Deal in Area" markers.
    - **Financing Clarity:** Monthly installment is always visible above the fold.
    - **Competition Stack:** Sellers are stacked to provide the "I have options" psychological trigger.
- **RFQ System:** Non-chat 'Request Better Price' flow with structured budget/timeline inputs for industrial buyers.

## III. OPERATIONAL & DATA TAXONOMY
- **Industrial Depth:** Production lines mapped by [Capacity, Power, Raw Materials, Import Status].
- **Linking:** Each material shows "Used in Machines", each machine shows "Required Materials" (Alibaba-style B2B flow).
- **Auto-Normalization:** Pipelines detect duplicates via pg_trgm, auto-correct brands, and validate media quality (reject <500px, reject duplicates).
- **Verification:** Inspection reports (Cars) and Location/ROI verification (Real Estate) are mandatory for high-value listings.

## IV. FINANCIAL & MONETIZATION ENGINE
- **Dual-Mode Calculation Engine:**
    - **Islamic:** [Base Price] + [Fixed Margin] / [Months]. No interest display.
    - **Conventional:** [Base Price] - [Down Payment] + [Reducing Interest Rate] / [Months].
- **Billing Ledger:**
    - **CPL Logic:** 5 EGP/WhatsApp, 7 EGP/Call. Deducted from 'Internal Wallet'.
    - **Fraud Protection:** 60-second gating, IP fingerprinting, and interaction-dwell duration validation.
- **Tiers:** Individual (3 free), Dealer/Industrial (Subscription tiers: Basic/Pro/Enterprise).

## V. DEALER OS & ADMIN HUB (The Control Center)
- **Dealer OS:** Bulk CSV/XML Import, Lead CRM, and real-time ROI/Analytics Dashboard.
- **Admin 'God-Mode':** - Moderation Queue for Industrial/Real Estate.
    - Revenue Heatmaps & Fraud Detection (too many listings/repeated phones).
    - **Global Kill-Switch:** Total operation pause for emergency/maintenance.
- **Audit Logs:** Every admin action (Delete/Ban/Approve) is timestamped and logged.

## VI. TECHNICAL STACK & PRODUCTION STANDARDS
- **Performance:** FlashList (Mobile), #000000 Forced Dark Mode, Next.js (Dealer OS).
- **Auth & Security:** Email OTP + Social Login (LinkedIn/Google/FB) + Biometric (FaceID/Fingerprint).
- **Retention:** Notifications on price drops, saved searches, and 'New Listings' for specific industrial requirements.
- **Compliance:** Full automated account deletion and legal data-safety disclosures per Google/Play requirements.

No shortcuts—build the digital infrastructure for the next generation of asset management."
