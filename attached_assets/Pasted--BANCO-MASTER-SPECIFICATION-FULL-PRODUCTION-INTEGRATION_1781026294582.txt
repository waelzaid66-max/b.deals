### B. User & Identity System (V22 Integration)
- **Profile:** Identity, Verification Badge, "My Listings" CRM, "Wallet/Transactions".
- **Social Layer:** Dealer/Company "Posts" (Offers/Updates) within their profile.
- **Security:** Biometric Auth (Expo Local Auth), JWT session handling, Device Tracking.

### C. The Conversion & Billing Engine (Monetization)
- **Limit Engine:** 3 free listings/month for individuals -> 50 EGP/day after.
- **CPL Engine:** `WhatsApp/Call` clicks = deduction from `User Wallet`.
- **Billing Integrity:** Every lead event must be atomic (Async track -> Billing Deduction -> Log).

### D. Operations & Admin (The "God Mode")
- **Moderation Queue:** Approval system for High-Value Industrial/Real Estate listings.
- **Support System:** Ticket management (Escalation alerts if reply > 24hrs).
- **Ops Dashboard:** Revenue Heatmaps, Lead Conversion rates, Global "Kill Switch".

### E. Mobile & Search UX
- **Deep Linking:** `banco://listing/:id` handling.
- **Search:** Support slang-detection (Arabic/English), price extraction, and nested category filtering.
- **UI:** #000000 Dark Mode global force, `SmartAssetCard` with Conversion Badge System, Sticky CTAs.
